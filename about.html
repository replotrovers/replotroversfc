About Replot Rovers

More than a football club.

Built From The Heart Of The Archipelago

Replot Rovers FC is a community football club being built from the ground up in Replot, Finland.

Our first men's team will take the field in 2027, but the project is about far more than football.

We believe football can bring people together, strengthen communities and create opportunities for people from all backgrounds.

That's why Replot Rovers is being built as a club rooted in community, sustainability, multiculturalism and local pride.

Why We Exist

Replot has incredible natural beauty, a strong local identity and a growing population.

Yet there is currently no men's football team representing the community and no regular football-centred meeting place bringing people together.

Replot Rovers aims to help fill that gap.

Through football, events, youth activities and partnerships, we want to create something that belongs to the community and grows with it.

Our Values

  • Community – People first, football second.
  • Sustainability – Building responsibly for the future.
  • Inclusion – Everyone is welcome.
  • Respect – On and off the pitch.
  • Ambition – Always striving to improve.

The Journey Starts Now

We are currently building the foundations of the club ahead of our planned 2027 launch.

Players, volunteers, supporters, sponsors and community partners are all invited to be part of the journey from the very beginning.

Because the best football clubs aren't bought.

They're built together.
